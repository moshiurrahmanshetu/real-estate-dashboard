Architectural Description

Discover an unparalleled level of coastal luxury living in the crown jewel of Beverly Hills. The Grand Peninsula Vista Villa represents a masterpiece of modern architecture, utilizing natural premium materials, expansive glass panels, and a seamless blend of indoor/outdoor entertainment spaces that cascade out to panoramic vistas. Set back on a highly exclusive 0.8-acre site, this 2024 structural achievement has been built to commercial-grade standards, and features five meticulously crafted bedroom suites and six bath layouts.

Entering the private security gates, you are welcomed by an architectural concrete drive with lush perimeter landscaping and mature specimen trees. The double-height grand foyer opens immediately to water views, featuring structural frameless glass and a central custom gold spiral stair. The main living level showcases dynamic marble floors, imported lighting features, and fully pocketing automated sliding doors opening directly to the 80-foot infinite heated pool, outdoor spa, custom fire pit terrace, and complete outdoor kitchen with automated weather shielding.

The epicurean primary kitchen is customized with professional Gaggenau appliances, handcrafted walnut cabinetry, and a private back-of-house preparatory butler pantry. The master wing occupies an entire upper section with distinct luxury lounge, complete dual-sided boutique dressing rooms, absolute private massage studio, and a massive wraparound outdoor private terrace. Premium amenities include a fully equipped home cinema with acoustic modeling, a private gym with specialized wellness recovery features, and complete integrated smart home automation.
